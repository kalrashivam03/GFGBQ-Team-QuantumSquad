Verify AI Responses. Detect Hallucinations. Trust the Output.

An AI-powered system that detects hallucinated facts and verifies citations in AI-generated content to improve accuracy, reliability, and trust.

Hallucination Detection

Identifies AI-generated statements that are likely incorrect or unsupported.

Citation Verification

Flags fake, invalid, or irrelevant citations generated by AI models.

Confidence Scoring

Assigns a confidence score to measure factual reliability of responses.

Source Suggestions

Recommends verified sources when hallucinations or fake citations are detected.

How It Works

1. Paste AI Content

User submits AI-generated text.

2. Analyze Claims

System extracts factual statements and citations.

3. Verify Sources

Claims are checked against trusted references.

4. Get Results

Hallucinations and fake citations are highlighted.
